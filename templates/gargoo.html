The Gargoo Meadow

GARGOO MEADOW

Explore the stunning beauty of the Gargoo Meadow in the Bagrote Valley.

Location

  • The Gargoo Meadow is located in the Bagrote Valley in Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan.
  • It is situated at an altitude of 3,000 meters (9,842 feet) above sea level.

    • Height and Climbing History

  • The Gargoo Meadow is a popular destination for trekkers and nature lovers.
  • It offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

    • Geography and Climate

  • The Gargoo Meadow is known for its lush greenery and diverse flora and fauna.
  • The climate in the meadow is cool and pleasant, making it an ideal spot for picnics and camping.

    • How to Get There

  • The Gargoo Meadow can be reached by road from Gilgit, the nearest major town.
  • It is a scenic drive through the Bagrote Valley, with breathtaking views of the Karakoram mountains.
